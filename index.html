----ABOUT THIS PROGRAM----

The Computing Security major prepares students to design, implement, and manage secure computing environments. It combines principles from computer science, information technology, and criminal justice to address modern cybersecurity challenges. Students learn how to protect digital assets, detect vulnerabilities, respond to cyber incidents, and ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.

----PROJECTS MADE BY STUDENTS----

Smart bin

This smart bin automatically opens when any motion or object is detected within the set distance range, the purpose of this smart bin is to make an easier, more hygenic and more enviromently friendly substitute of the average trash bins.

Attendance checker

This attendance checker stores the attendance of each student after they enter their assinged pin on the keypad, which makes it easier and faster to take and manage attendance. It helps keep accurate records, saves time, and reduces mistakes.

Sound sensor rgb

The Sound Sensor RGP detects noise and sends a signal when it hears a sound above a set level. It can be used in projects like sound-activated lights or alarms.
